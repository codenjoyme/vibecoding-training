# Prompt Engineering Toolkit — Techniques for Navigating AI Knowledge

**Duration:** 5-7 minutes  
**Skill:** Apply 15 prompt engineering techniques to unlock deeper, more relevant AI responses from a blank chat

**👉 [Start hands-on walkthrough](walkthrough.md)**

## Topics

- The Mirror metaphor — AI reflects your language, framing, and expertise level back at you
- The Room of Requirement metaphor — you can't ask for what you don't know exists
- Techniques for discovering what you don't know you don't know
- Role assignment and audience framing
- Chain-of-thought and step-by-step reasoning
- Few-shot examples and output format control
- Requesting alternatives and adversarial critique
- Reverse prompting and meta-learning about questions
- Iterative refinement and Socratic questioning
- Building a personal prompt toolkit for any new chat session

## Learning Outcome

Ability to choose and apply the right prompting technique to navigate AI toward the knowledge corpus where real solutions live — regardless of your own expertise level

## When to Use

- Starting a new chat session with no context loaded
- Working in an unfamiliar domain where you don't know the right terminology
- Getting answers that feel shallow, generic, or aimed at the wrong audience
- Wanting to push AI beyond surface-level responses into expert-level territory
- Needing to discover your own blind spots before making decisions

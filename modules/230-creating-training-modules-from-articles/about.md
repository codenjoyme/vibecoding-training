# Creating Training Modules from Articles

**Duration:** 15 minutes

**Skill:** Transform any article, blog post, or technical resource about GenAI into a structured, course-quality training module by feeding it to an AI agent with creation instructions

**👉 [Start hands-on walkthrough](walkthrough.md)**

## Topics

- Course module structure: `about.md` + `walkthrough.md` anatomy and required sections
- Source material preparation: extracting the teachable concept from an article
- The "module factory" prompt: combining source material with creation instructions
- Quality review checklist: prerequisites format, understanding questions, cross-platform paths
- Integration workflow: training plan updates, folder naming, Git commit
- Fork & PR contribution workflow for shared course repositories

## Learning Outcome

You will take a real article about a GenAI topic and produce a complete, course-quality training module from it — with proper `about.md`, `walkthrough.md`, correct prerequisites, hands-on exercises, and understanding questions. You will integrate the module into the training plan and learn the fork & PR workflow for contributing modules to shared repositories.

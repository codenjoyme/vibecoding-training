# AI Cost Optimization & Token Economics

**Duration:** 15 minutes

**Skill:** Measure, understand, and reduce AI API costs using token economics and model routing strategies.

**👉 [Start hands-on walkthrough](walkthrough.md)**

## Topics

- What tokens are and how LLM pricing works (input vs. output)
- Reading your Copilot and API usage dashboards
- Cost reduction techniques: context trimming, prompt compression, caching
- Model routing: cheap models for simple tasks, expensive models for hard ones
- Budget estimation for a team of N people

## Learning Outcome

You can read an AI usage bill, identify where money is going, apply at least three cost reduction techniques, and produce a monthly budget estimate for your team's AI tooling.

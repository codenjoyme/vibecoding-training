# Evaluating AI Output Quality

**Duration:** 15 minutes

**Skill:** Apply a systematic framework to assess AI output for correctness, completeness, and hallucination risk — and decide when to trust, verify, or reject.

**👉 [Start hands-on walkthrough](walkthrough.md)**

## Topics

- The trust spectrum: when to trust, verify, or reject AI output
- Hallucination detection patterns: confident but wrong, plausible but fabricated
- Practical scoring: rate AI responses on correctness, completeness, relevance, safety
- Cross-validation technique: comparing two models to surface uncertainty
- Building a reusable quality checklist for your team

## Learning Outcome

You can score any AI response objectively, recognise the most common hallucination patterns, and produce a reusable quality assessment checklist your team can apply to every critical AI output.

### Required Modules

- [080 — Learning from Hallucinations](../080-learning-from-hallucinations/about.md)

# Course Feedback & Completion

**Duration:** 5-7 minutes

**Skill:** Reflect on your learning journey, provide structured feedback on the training course, and send it to the course author for continuous improvement.

**👉 [Start hands-on walkthrough](walkthrough.md)**

## Topics

- Reflecting on skills acquired throughout the course
- Structuring constructive feedback for maximum impact
- Identifying the most and least valuable modules
- Suggesting improvements and new topics
- Sending feedback via email to course maintainers

## Learning Outcome

You will have reflected on the complete training journey, articulated what worked well and what needs improvement, and sent detailed feedback to the course author. Your feedback directly shapes future versions of this course.

## Prerequisites

### Required Modules

None — this module can be completed at any point, but it is designed as the final module after completing the full course.

### Required Skills & Tools

- Completed at least several modules from the course
- Email client or ability to send email from terminal
- A few minutes to reflect on your experience

## When to Use

- After completing the full training course
- After completing a significant portion of the modules
- When you want to share suggestions for course improvement
- When your team finishes the training and you want to consolidate group feedback

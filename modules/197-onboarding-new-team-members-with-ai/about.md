# Onboarding New Team Members with AI

**Duration:** 15 minutes

**Skill:** Set up an AI-powered onboarding system — project-aware instructions, codebase Q&A, and SpecKit documentation — so a new team member can answer "how does X work?" in minutes, not days.

**👉 [Start hands-on walkthrough](walkthrough.md)**

## Topics

- Why traditional onboarding fails and what makes AI-assisted onboarding different
- Creating a project-aware onboarding instruction that explains structure, conventions, and decisions
- Using AI for codebase Q&A: "where is X?", "how does Y work?", "why was Z chosen?"
- Connecting SpecKit documentation as a grounded knowledge base for AI answers
- Simulating the new-joiner experience and measuring onboarding quality

## Learning Outcome

You can deploy an AI-powered onboarding setup that lets a new team member ask natural-language questions about the codebase and get accurate, grounded answers — reducing time-to-productivity from weeks to days.

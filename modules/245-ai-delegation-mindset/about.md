# AI Delegation Mindset for Team Leads

**Duration:** 45-60 minutes  
**Skill:** Delegate one real engineering task per day to an AI agent, then verify outcomes with lightweight oversight that avoids both micromanagement and blind trust

**👉 [Start hands-on walkthrough](walkthrough.md)**

## Topics

- Why delegation is a senior/team-lead skill and why AI amplifies it
- The 1-task-per-day habit for building delegation muscle over 30 days
- Mindset shift: from doing tasks yourself to designing a system that executes them
- Writing delegation briefs with context, constraints, and acceptance criteria
- Verification without micromanagement: what to check manually vs what to automate
- Building a self-sustaining loop: task → brief → agent → verification → feedback → instruction update
- Red flags: when to step in directly vs when to improve the system
- Old way vs AI delegation way across common team-lead scenarios

## Learning Outcome

You can pick one real daily task that fits AI delegation, write a clear delegation brief, run it through an AI agent, and verify the result with a lightweight checklist while maintaining oversight without micromanaging.

## When to Use

- When you are spending too much time on repetitive implementation or coordination tasks
- When AI outputs feel risky and you need a reliable verification routine
- When mentoring senior developers who need leadership-level AI delegation habits
- When scaling team throughput without lowering quality standards
